# Morocco Discovery - Project Brief & PRD (Updated)

## 1. Project Overview
Morocco Discovery is a premium travel platform designed to help travelers discover the hidden gems, exquisite dining, and cultural heritage of Morocco. The brand emphasizes luxury, authenticity, and discovery through a "Modern Moorish" aesthetic.

## 2. Brand Identity
- **Primary Color:** Moroccan Gold (`#d4af37`) - Symbolizes luxury, heritage, and the Sahara sunset.
- **Secondary Color:** Deep Moroccan Green (`#146c3c`) - Represents nature, tradition, and lush oases.
- **Typography:** EB Garamond (Serif) for headlines and editorial content; clean Sans-Serif for body copy and navigation.
- **Design Philosophy:** Editorial layouts, high-fidelity photography, and a balance of whitespace to create a sense of breathability and premium service.

## 3. Key Features
### A. Immersive Discovery
- **Destinations Hub:** A curated collection of Moroccan cities (e.g., Marrakesh, Chefchaouen, Essaouira, Fes) acting as entry points to dynamic city guides.
- **Dynamic City Guides:** Immersive `/destinations/[slug]` routes featuring cinematic hero sections, "Heartbeat of the City" editorial storytelling, and "Traveler Essentials."
- **Visual Exploration:** High-impact hero sections, immersive photography, and editorial-style typography.

### B. Specialized Listings
- **Fine Dining:** Filterable directory of high-end restaurants categorized by cuisine (Moroccan, French, Fusion, Street Food), location, and neighborhood.
- **Curated Accommodations:** Discovery-led listings for premium stays, including Riads, Boutique Hotels, and Luxury Desert Camps.

### C. Functional Utilities
- **Advanced Filtering:** Persistent sidebars (Desktop) and category chips (Mobile) for refining results by type, price range, and amenities.
- **Conversion Points:** Integrated "Book Now," "Book Table," and "Plan My Trip" calls-to-action throughout the user journey.
- **Cross-Device Navigation:** Seamless transition between desktop top-bar navigation and mobile bottom-bar exploration.

## 4. Technical Specifications
- **Design System:** "Modern Moorish" — Standardized tokens for colors, spacing (Round 8), and typography (EB Garamond).
- **Responsiveness:** Optimized for Desktop and Mobile (iOS/Android) with consistent feature parity.
- **Route Architecture:** Support for dynamic routing (e.g., `/destinations/:slug`).

## 5. Screen Inventory
1.  **Landing Page (Desktop/Mobile):** Immersive entry point with seasonal highlights and primary discovery pathways.
2.  **Destinations Hub (Desktop/Mobile):** Visual grid of iconic cities for exploration.
3.  **Destination Details (Desktop/Mobile):** Immersive template for city-specific guides (e.g., Marrakesh).
4.  **Restaurant Listing (Desktop/Mobile):** Searchable and filterable dining directory.
5.  **Accommodation Listing (Desktop/Mobile):** Curated directory of Riads, hotels, and camps.
6.  **Login Page (Desktop/Mobile):** Elegant authentication flow featuring atmospheric brand imagery.

## 6. Success Metrics
- Increase in multi-category exploration (Destinations → Accommodations).
- High engagement with "Traveler Essentials" modules.
- Conversion rate on booking-related CTAs.
